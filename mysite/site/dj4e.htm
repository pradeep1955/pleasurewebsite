About Me

I am a retired electrical engineer who graduated from Government Engineering College Bilaspur in 1978. I joined MPEB in 1979 as a Graduate apprentice. After the state bifurcation in November 2000, our organization was renamed as CSEB and later as CSPTCL under CSEB.

Most of my service period was in the construction and maintenance of transmission lines in Chhattisgarh. I thoroughly enjoyed my work. It was a team effort, and my colleagues and senior officers understood each other and were ready to help out in any difficult time. I retired as Executive Director in 2017.

Since my retirement, I have developed a keen interest in computers and data science. The creation of this site is also a result of my keen interest in this field.
